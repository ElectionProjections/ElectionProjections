Adjust national vote shares to see a projected seat distribution for Italy's Chamber of Deputies under the Rosatellum — the mixed system currently in force. About 37% of seats go to first-past-the-post winners in 147 single-member constituencies; the rest are proportional, calculated nationally with a 3% threshold for parties and 10% for coalitions.

About the thresholds

Solo parties need 3% nationally to win proportional seats; coalitions need 10% combined. Inside a winning coalition, a member polling 1–3% gets no seats of its own — its votes pass proportionally to coalition partners above 3%. Below 1%, votes are simply lost.

Method. The Rosatellum elects 400 deputies three ways: 147 in single-member constituencies by plurality (FPTP), 245 in proportional seats calculated nationally, and 8 from constituencies abroad. Proportional seats use the official two-stage process — Hare quota with largest remainders, first among coalitions/parties that clear the threshold, then within each coalition among its qualifying lists — which should land within a seat or two of the certified 2022 figures (a further regional-distribution step in the real law isn't modelled here). FPTP seats can't be modelled district-by-district the way Poland's PR seats were, since only coalition-level results by region are published, not vote shares for all 147 races — so this tool uses a national swing model calibrated to reproduce the actual 2022 coalition-level outcome (centre-right 121, centre-left 12, M5S 10, regional parties 4) exactly, then scales from there. Seats inside each coalition are split using the actual 2022 candidacy allocation between partners, since that's negotiated rather than vote-proportional. South Tyrol's People's Party (3 seats), South calls North (1), Aosta Valley (1), and the Associative Movement of Italians Abroad (1) are fixed at their 2022 levels — fields too regionally specific for a national model — alongside the 7 overseas seats won by PD, FdI, and M5S in 2022.

Sources. 2022 results: Italian Ministry of the Interior, via Wikipedia's certified results breakdown.
